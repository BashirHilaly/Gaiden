    Why Philosophy?

    We decided to make a website on philosophy due to the fact that not many people are knowledgeable on the subject, whether it be because they are too busy or just not interested. The main complaint we received from our survey we conducted was that philosophy is too complicated and boring, so we wanted a way to make learning it more accessible and interactive.

    What are we trying to achieve

    We feel as though many students in this day and age struggle to find purpose and have nothing to guide them through life, especially with constant schoolwork and struggles at home. We want to help educate such people with prompt information on different perspectives on life through the learning of major figures and the history of philosophy, while giving empty templates to make his or her own interpretations.

    We hope

    that finding purpose in life is given more importance especially socially. It appears as a "too deep" topic and "not fun" but this should not prevent people, especially young students who need it, from not thinking.
